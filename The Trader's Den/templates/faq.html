Why use the Trader's Den?

With the rise of communities around the internet, it has become a must for every investor and trader to take the public sentiment towards a company into consideration. As we have all seen in the $GME craze, outstanding and parabolic returns can be made by riding those dynamic trends. The Trader's Den offers an easy and simplified way of accessing the public sentiment on reddit towards a specific stock ticker.

How to use subreddit search?

The subreddit search is a tool to compile the daily hottest threads on a given subreddit. This tool will allow you to specify a certain subreddit and extract the title with a hyperlink to the 'n' top threads with the most upvotes. The hyperlink can be used by clicking on the given entry.

How to use stock search

In the stock search page, you can write any stock ticker of your choice and view its graph/ market info/ number of mentions in 'wallstreetbets' (bottom right) If it is your first time entering the ticker, expect a moderate wait of approx 17 seconds as fetching the number of mentions for a stock ticker takes time due to the sheer amount of comments and posts. (Although there is a certain limit on the amount of posts that can be analysed daily). This value is then cached in your cookies so incase you want to access it again it will be much faster to fetch.

Where do you get all your data from ?

All the subreddit data stems from reddit PRAW API which acts as a wrapper for the original reddit API. All this data is compiled live from reddit with the given constraints given by you our user. All stock related data is obtained from yahoo finance via the get_stock_ticker method. This allows us to fetch real time data given by a reliable source (yahoo).
